About

Fencepost Vases opened for business in early 2004. It's grown exponentially since then, and has become the country's largest wholesale manufacturer of bud vases. All products are made from recycled barbed-wire fence posts. Most times the posts have aged 50 years or more and are full of character.

We are a family-owned business that prides ourselves in quality and value. If you ever have any problems with any of our products, feel free to contact us. We will either refund your money or replace the defective product (your choice).
