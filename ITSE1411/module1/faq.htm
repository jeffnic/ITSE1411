Frequently Asked Questions

How are the Bud Vases made?
We select solid heart cedar and turn them round on a wood lathe. They are then bored and a special plastic sleeve is inserted into the hole to hold water.

How long will my order take?
This depends on the size of the order and our current backlog. Typically orders ship in 2 to 4 weeks. Contact us if you need them sooner.

What kind of oil should I get for the oil candles?
Look for oil that is clean burning and smoke free. We've always had good luck with the Walmart brand.

Where do you find the fence posts?
We've developed a relationship with several ranchers in Central Texas who are more than happy to sell us their used barbed-wire fence posts.
