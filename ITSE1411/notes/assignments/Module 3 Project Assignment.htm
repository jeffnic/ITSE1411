ITSE 1411 Beginning Web
Module 3 Project Assignment
Assignment
  1. Update your restaurant site with a new window
    • Add a link called specials to your restaurant site that opens a new window when clicked.
    • Your specials page should list the current specials with images and have a close window link.
  2. Create a banner ad for your home page.
    • Your banner ad should contain 2 different images that change at an interval of 2 seconds (2000 milliseconds).
  3. Update index page with links
    • Open your index.htm page in Komodo Edit.
    • Copy all pages from your module2 folder that you need to properly display your new restaurant site to your module3 folder.
    • Update your index.htm page to link to your project3.htm page in your module3 folder.
    • Upload your index.htm page to the public_html folder and your project3.htm in your Module3 folder. Test for validation.
    • Email jscholl@austincc.edu for grading.
